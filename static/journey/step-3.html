River Irk at Cheetham Hill

9:30am Wednesday 11 January 2017

Flooding is expected - immediate action required

Flood warning in force now

We are expecting flooding of property close to the river bank on Danzic Street leading onto the southern end of Collyhurst Road. A peak of 1.7m is forecast at 2am on Friday 13 January 2017.

Locations affected are Danzic Street leading onto Collyhurst Road area of Salford.

We will continue to monitor levels closely and update this message as necessary.

This information was last updated at 9:15am Wednesday 11 January 2017
