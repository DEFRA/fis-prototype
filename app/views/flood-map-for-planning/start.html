Flood map for planning

Use this service to find which flood zone your location is in.

Before you start

This service has 3 steps:

  1. Give a postcode, place, National Grid Reference or easting and northing co-ordinates for your location
  2. Confirm the location on a map
  3. See the flood zones in and around your location

Find the location
Easting and northing For example, 123456 123456
